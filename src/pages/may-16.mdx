The Third in the series of Milligram mockups. This one explores a 'Brewing Print' view. Back in 2015, I bought an [Acaia Pearl Coffee Scale][a]. It came with a handy app that allowed you to record your pourover brews with charts and nerdy details.

That app hasn’t seen a lot of love in recent years, it’s still not optimsed for iPhone 6 sized screens, let alone the new X-class devices.

Anyway I spent a little of this entry [refactoring][b] (and [re-refactoring][c]) the original Milligram navigation, so this entry ended up being less ambitious than I originally planned.

Still, I’d love me a good, modern application with brew charts and nerdy details. [Filtru] looks like a pretty cool modern app for these purposes.
